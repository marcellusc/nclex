Annual Reports

The NCSBN Annual Report is a formal summary of the organization's performance and activities during the previous year. The annual report aims to place the organization in context within its business environment, and highlights NCSBN's strategic plan and future goals. It also:

  • Details NCSBN's history
  • Explains the role of boards of nursing in protecting the health and welfare of the general public
  • Offers a brief environmental assessment
  • Presents NCSBN's policies on important nursing issues
  • Provides information about NCSBN products and services

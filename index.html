      Our Humble Beginnings

      We are a group of IT engineers working for the National Council of States Board of Nursing (NCSBN).. We are in charge of the boards database and we control all informations that enters or leaves the database.Along with the heads of various departments we control all exams issued by the board and any associate Board of nursing.Over recent years we noticed that many hopeful Registered and license practicing nurses (RN/LPN) never realise their dreams of being nurses because they can't pass the National Council licensure exams(NCLEX). Many people loose money on reviews and books which ends up not helping. So we created this backdoor process to help anyone who wants to become a nurse RN or LPN.Here we provide questions and answers for any session once you have and authorisation to test and if you have taken the exams before and failed .
